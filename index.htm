Themed events

Themed events are gatherings or occasions where the decor, activities, and sometimes even the dress code are centered around a specific theme. These themes can range from specific time periods, such as the 1920s or the 1980s, to concepts like masquerade balls, tropical luau parties, or even specific movies or TV shows.
